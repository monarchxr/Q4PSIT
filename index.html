About this site and the reason behind it

Welcome to this question bank for PSIT Kanpur that I've created.

It serves as a medium to share previous year question papers of PSIT, which include Assignments(if possible), Class Tests(CT-1/2/3) and Pre-University(PU).

When I joined PSIT, I knew I had to keep my academics on point. So I decided to ask seniors for previous year question papers. But as we all know, no one keeps the question paper after the test is over.

In order to fix that, I have (or I am currently building) built this website as a medium to share previous year question papers with other students! P.S. I'm also learning web development through this
